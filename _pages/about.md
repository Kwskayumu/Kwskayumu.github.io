I am a Master's student in Aeronautics and Astronautics at the University of Tokyo,
working in the Astrodynamics and Space Systems Laboratory (津田・森・佐伯研) under
Prof. Yuichi Tsuda.
I received my B.Eng. in Aeronautics and Astronautics from the University of Tokyo in 2025.
As an undergraduate, I worked on swarm-robot exploration algorithms, and in my
Master's program I am focusing on relative GNC for deep-space missions. Through
projects at the University of Tokyo and the Institute of Space and Astronautical
Science (ISAS/JAXA), I have also contributed to a range of missions, including
the development and operations of small satellites and concept studies for the
next-generation small-body sample-return mission.

Research Interests
======
Astrodynamics · Optical navigation · Initial orbit determination · Small-body proximity operations ·
Spacecraft guidance, navigation, and control · Rendezvous and docking
